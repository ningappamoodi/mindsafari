Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindSafari ("we," "our," or "us"). MindSafari is an Android application that offers fun, AI-powered cognitive games designed for children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you or your child uses the MindSafari app and our website at https://ningappamoodi.github.io/mindsafari/.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users — especially children. We design our app to collect as little information as possible.

2. Information We Collect

MindSafari is designed to minimize data collection. We may collect:

  • Gameplay data (on device): game progress, scores, and difficulty settings. This information is stored locally on the device and is used only to provide the game experience.
  • Anonymous usage and diagnostic data: aggregated, non-identifying information such as crash reports and app performance metrics, used solely to improve stability and quality.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or other contact details from children
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or other personal files on the device

3. Children's Privacy

MindSafari is intended for use by children under parental supervision. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions relating to children.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require account creation to play.
  • The app contains no third-party advertising and no social features that allow contact with strangers.
  • Any AI features operate on-device or process only anonymized, non-personal inputs.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@sanvira.ai and we will promptly delete it.

4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the app's game features
  • Save game progress and adapt difficulty on the device
  • Diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • Improve game design and overall app quality

We never use collected information for advertising, profiling, or marketing to children.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share personal information with third parties. Anonymous, aggregated diagnostic data may be processed by the service providers listed below strictly to operate and improve the app. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Third-Party Services

MindSafari may rely on the following categories of third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: app distribution and updates, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Crash reporting and analytics: anonymized crash and performance data used only to keep the app stable.

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use data from MindSafari for their own purposes.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including keeping gameplay data on the device, using secure (HTTPS) connections for any network communication, and limiting the data we collect in the first place. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, but minimizing collection is our strongest safeguard.

8. Permissions

MindSafari requests only the permissions necessary to function:

  • Internet access: used for app updates and anonymous diagnostics.

The app does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, location, or external storage. If future features require additional permissions, we will request them transparently and update this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, provide a prominent notice within the app or on our website. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, contact us:

  • Email: contact@sanvira.ai
  • Website: https://ningappamoodi.github.io/mindsafari/
